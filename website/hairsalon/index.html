Courses / Career

School of Hair Dressing

Olivia's School of Hair Dressing / Barbering is an approved certificate qualification. This course has a class duration of 12 weeks and offers a certificate qualification upon completion. Our classes are small and pay individual attention to each student and offers a high standard of tuition. The course is designed to prepare students for the work place, it covers a wide variety of practical work in a live environment.

Qualifications are based on an ongoing assessment. Any person of any age may apply to do this course, it is a fantastic introduction into a career of hair dressing, which is a very rewarding career path. If you are interested in obtaining this qualification, please contact me through my contact page. Alternatively you can call the number provided for more details or questions you may have.

Subjects covered on this course include:

Client Care
Consultation
Health & Safety
Shampooing
Hair Types
Technique
Using utilities
Scissors razor
Clippers
Blending
Styling
Blow Drying
